COMMON QUESTIONS

My agent said a standard inspection is fine.
A standard covers 23-35 visible items. No infrared, sewer camera, or foundation plotting. The expensive problems are the ones those tools find.
What if the house is in good shape?
Even though a house may be in good condition and ready to buy, there are likely many maintenance and a few hidden defects that you'll want to know about before the house becomes yours.
Why choose Comprehensive over Standard?
A standard inspection tells you what's visible to the eye. Comprehensive covers 7 high-risk categories others miss and prices the repairs — so you can negotiate the best deal before you close.
